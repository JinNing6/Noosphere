# Namespace Policy / 命名空间策略

> **Last Updated: 2026-03-19**

## Overview / 概述

Noosphere maintains a small set of reserved package names on PyPI to protect the ecosystem from supply chain attacks (dependency confusion, typosquatting).
These packages are currently placeholder-only (`0.0.1`) and will be populated with real functionality as the project evolves.

Noosphere 在 PyPI 上维护了一组保留的包名，以保护项目生态免受供应链攻击（依赖混淆、拼写抢注）。
这些包目前仅为占位符（`0.0.1`），将随着项目演进逐步注入真正的功能。

## What We Released / 我们释放了什么

In March 2026, we proactively released ~80 generic and cultural namespace reservations back to the public PyPI ecosystem, including names drawn from popular media franchises and general philosophical concepts.

2026 年 3 月，我们主动将约 80 个通用及文化概念类的命名空间释放回公共 PyPI 生态，包括来自流行影视作品和通用哲学概念的名称。

**We believe open-source namespaces belong to the community, not to any single project.**

**我们认为开源命名空间属于社区，而非任何单一项目。**
